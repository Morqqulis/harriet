About

Become AI security–ready in just 8 weeks. Gain the skills  to launch or advance your career in AI security — whatever your  background. By the end of the course, you’ll rethink what it means to be “technical”: even if you’ve never written a line of code before, you’ll be running AI attacks and understanding how to defend against them.

Who should take this course?

  • Level 1 Foundation

    Open to everyone. Perfect for professionals in any field who want to understand the risks of AI, while still getting hands-on with technical exercises.

  • Level 2 Strategic

    Designed for CISOs, risk managers, and leaders who need to integrate AI security into governance and risk frameworks.

  • Level 3 practitioner

    For cyber and AI security professionals who want advanced technical proficiency. You must complete each level in order — no shortcuts.

What you’ll gain

  1. 1

    Skills to get hired in AI security roles across industry, government, and research

  2. 2

    Hands-on technical expertise, regardless of your starting point. You’ll leave knowing how to run attacks against AI systems and how to defend  them

  3. 3

    A shareable certificate to showcase on LinkedIn and with your network

  4. 4

    A global community of peers to support your career long after the course ends

What’s included

  1. 1

    8 weeks of blended learning: self-paced e-learning modules you can fit around your schedule plus weekly live facilitator-led seminars (multiple time zone options) to do exercises, ask questions, and cement your knowledge

  2. 2

    Deliberately small cohorts to ensure personalised attention and quality outcomes

  3. 3

    Access to an exclusive alumni network — stay connected to future leaders in AI security
